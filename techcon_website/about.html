History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of software developers passionate about innovation. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the largest global conferences focused on emerging technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower technology enthusiasts, professionals, and organizations to collaborate and shape the future through innovation, inclusion, and creativity.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted visionary speakers from around the world — from startup founders to industry pioneers. Each has contributed to making TechCon a platform of inspiration and learning.
